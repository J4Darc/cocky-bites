Why Cocky Bites?

The mission of Cocky Bites is to give students an honest and entertaining guide to campus dining by sharing our personal reviews, ratings, and recommendations. Cocky Bites serves as hub for all campus dining resources, while serving as a safe space for hoest feedback. Students have the opportunity to actively give their feedback on the dining options and be an active contributor to the dining community. We hope this will spark collaboration with UofSC dining staff and students, where students get to be a part of the process of curating the experience they would like to see.
