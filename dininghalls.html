Garnet Station

Garnet Station, the new all-you-care-to-eat dining hall, is built to satisfy every craving. Grab sizzling burgers at the grill, explore flavorful dishes at the Global Station, or build your perfect plate at the fresh salad bar. With a True Balance station for allergen-conscious meals, there’s truly something for everyone at Garnet Station.
