Campus Eats, Cocky Critiques

Bite In, Gamecocks

Hungry on campus? We’ve got you covered. Cocky Bites is your go-to guide for all things dining at the University of South Carolina. From honest reviews of dining halls and campus restaurants to tips and tricks for making the most of your meal plan, we’re here to help you find the best bites around. Whether you’re looking for a quick snack between classes, the best study spot with food, or hidden gems you didn’t know existed, Cocky Bites will point you in the right direction.

Latest Updates

Campus Village Pavillion

"The Pavilion (also known as the Path) will be opening at 9 am on Saturday game days!"

Garnet Station

What was once Gamecock Park has been re imagined into a brand-new dining space with 500 seats, seven stations, and both indoor and outdoor options. And here’s the twist: what used to be on the second floor is now right on the first — making it easier than ever to grab a bite.

Campus Restaurants

The Garden Grill is a patio-only dining spot, so its hours depend on the weather. It serves up burgers, sandwiches, and salads. Right now, the Garden Grill is closed, but it’s set to reopen in Summer 2026.

Blenz

What was previously Berry Bliss downstairs is now Blenz. They serve pitaya and açaí bowls with 5+ toppings, along with a variety of smoothies.

Dunkin Donuts

Dunkin’ Donuts is coming soon! Opening later in the Fall 2025 semester, it’ll be located in the Science and Technology Building — so you can grab your coffee (and a donut or two) right on the way to class.
