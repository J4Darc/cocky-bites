Bite In, Gamecocks

Hungry on campus? We’ve got you covered. Cocky Bites is your go-to guide for all things dining at the University of South Carolina. From honest reviews of dining halls and campus restaurants to tips and tricks for making the most of your meal plan, we’re here to help you find the best bites around. Whether you’re looking for a quick snack between classes, the best study spot with food, or hidden gems you didn’t know existed, Cocky Bites will point you in the right direction.

Latest Updates

Formerly located upstairs at Russell House, Gamecock Park has been moved downstairs and officially renamed Garnet Station — part of USC’s restructuring of dining traffic and capacity.
The former Gamecock Park now hosts Blenz, Cicci Di Carne, Congaree Craft Pizza, Gamecock Griddle, FoodLab (rotating), Stonie Bowls, and Union Grill — encouraging specialized dining options.
Previously opening at 10AM on Saturdays, Campus Village Pavilion now opens an hour earlier to accommodate game day student demand.
The planned Dunkin’ Donuts in the Science & Technology Building was expected Fall 2025 but has yet to be completed; USC has not issued an official statement regarding future opening.
